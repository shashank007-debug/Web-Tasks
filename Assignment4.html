Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha is a fictional character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a member of the Uchiha clan and is known for his exceptional skills as a ninja, particularly in genjutsu and ninjutsu. Itachi is also recognized for his tragic backstory, which involves sacrificing his own happiness for the sake of his younger brother, Sasuke Uchiha.

Itachi's character is complex, as he is initially portrayed as a villain but later revealed to have noble intentions. He is a member of the Akatsuki organization and plays a significant role in the series' overarching plot. Itachi's signature techniques include the Sharingan, Tsukuyomi, and Amaterasu.
